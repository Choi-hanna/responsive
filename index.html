  • Flat Design

    Flat design is an interface design style that emphasizes the minimal use of simple elements, typography, and flat colors.
    Designers may prefer flat designs because they can make interface designs more concise and efficient.
    It is easier to convey information quickly while making it visually appealing and accessible.

  • Dark Mode

    Dark mode is a mode in which a screen with a dark background appears on the user's smartphone interface, not a conventional bright screen.
    It is also called "night mode" because it is suitable for use in the evening to relieve eye fatigue.

  • White Space

    White space creates a starting environment so that visitors can focus on the specific action they want without feeling distracted from any elements.
    It also relaxes visitors' eyes and helps them understand by defining the relationship between page elements.

  • Grid

    A grid is a tool that organizes space and information and shows it to readers, a tool that is the basis of the entire work,
    and a rule that gives and maintains order.
    It is also applied to books, newspapers, various information magazines and reports, and websites.
